Program comparison

CSE courses on average hold a higher amount of students than that of INFO classes and the CSE department has more than double the amount of courses than the INFO department. Between having more classes, and more students on average per class, the CSE department comes out to be significantly larger than the INFO department. While being a larger department, the average GPA across all CSE courses is lower than that of INFO courses implying that CSE courses are likely more difficult to get good grades in compared to INFO courses. In summary, the CSE department is much larger than the INFO department but also is more difficult to succeed in compared to INFO.
